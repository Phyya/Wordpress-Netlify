What causes gas and bloating? The most common cause of gas and bloating after a tummy tuck is the change in your diet. Warning Signs After Tummy Tuck

A tummy tuck surgery is a major procedure. But the results are often well worth the effort. If you have had a tummy tuck, you will have some pain and swelling in the first few days after surgery. You may also experience some bruising and swelling of the incision sites.

Here are some warning signs after your tummy tuck:

You have pain or discomfort that does not go away with medication. Pain that is severe or lasts longer than three to four days could indicate an infection or other complication.

You notice redness, swelling or drainage from the incision sites. This could be a sign of infection or blood clots in the legs (deep vein thrombosis).

Your urine turns dark brown or red and smells bad. This means that there may be blood in your urine due to a kidney problem caused by constipation or straining during bowel movements (tenesmus).
