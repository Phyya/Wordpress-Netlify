Do Whitening Strips Work

Whitening strips can be an excellent substitute for professional bleaching or other whitening procedures. They are convenient, inexpensive, and simple to use. But do they really function?

What Are Whitening Strips’ Mechanisms?

A unique kind of plastic is molded into a thin sheet to create whitening strips. This plastic sheet is applied to your teeth for roughly 20 minutes while being covered in peroxide or carbamide peroxide gel. Throughout the course of this period, the gel will gradually release hydrogen peroxide into your mouth.

The peroxide will continue to act on your teeth after the strip is taken off for roughly an hour before it is totally absorbed into your mouth and loses its ability to whiten teeth.
