Types of laser hair removal

There are two types of laser hair removal:

Nd-YAG laser

This type of laser is commonly used in medical settings and has been cleared by the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for cosmetic use since 1997. It targets melanin (the pigment that gives skin its color) in the base of the hair follicle and destroys it, which prevents new hairs growing back.

Ruby laser

The ruby laser targets hemoglobin (a protein found in red blood cells) rather than melanin. This means it works on all skin colors but takes longer to treat each area because it doesn’t work as quickly as the Nd-YAG laser does.
