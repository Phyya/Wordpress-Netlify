Body Contouring Vs Tummy Tuck?

Tummy tucks and body contouring are two different things. You may have heard people talking about them in the same breath, but they’re actually very different procedures. Body contouring involves removing fat from a specific area of your body through liposuction or other means, while tummy tucks involve surgically removing fat and skin and tightening abdominal muscles to create a flatter stomach. You can have either procedure done without having the other one, but if you’re looking for something drastic and permanent (which is why most people choose these kinds of surgeries), it’s best to get both done at once.

The main difference between body contouring and tummy tuck is how the results are presented. Both can effectively reduce excess fat and give you a more toned, flat stomach. However, don’t assume that the two procedures are an exact replacement for one another. Contact a plastic surgeon to discuss whether a tummy tuck is right for you and learn about all the procedures available.
