Bulge After Tummy Tuck Surgery?

Tummy tuck surgery, also known as abdominoplasty, is a procedure that reshapes the skin and fat around the abdominal area. It can be done for cosmetic reasons, but it’s also often used to treat medical conditions such as panniculus (abdominal flab) and stretch marks. The procedure can be performed using several different methods, but in general involves cutting away extra skin and fat tissue, then sewing up the remaining skin to tighten it.
