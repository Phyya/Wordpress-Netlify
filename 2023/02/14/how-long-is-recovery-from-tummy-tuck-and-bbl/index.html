What is tummy tuck BBL?

A tummy tuck BBL is a comprehensive body contouring plastic surgery designed to create an appealing and youthful mid-body shape and size. Tummy tuck and BBL together usually produce far more dramatic results than just one of these surgeries performed independently. Here are some of the key benefits of this combined cosmetic surgery.

  • Complementary procedures: To create an aesthetically desirable look, you need less fat in your abdomen, and more fat in your buttocks. The shape and size of your front and rear end should complement each, which can be achieved perfectly with an hourglass tummy tuck and BBL surgery.
  • Less risk, more impact: A combined tummy tuck BBL procedure means that you will undergo general anesthesia only once and will have only one surgery and recovery process. So, your surgical risk is reduced, while effectiveness improves with a single procedure instead of two separate surgeries.
  • Customization benefits: Your plastic surgeon can perform your tummy tuck BBL in a customized manner by planning common incisions and tailoring the procedure to your unique anatomy. You will have more natural looking results and less scarring compared to a situation where both surgeries are performed separately.

How To Prepare For Tummy Tuck And BBL

At your pre-operative consultation, your plastic surgeon will provide you guidelines on how to prepare for tummy tuck and BBL. Your preparation ideally begins from the moment you have decided to go ahead with this surgery. Plan ahead of time for your surgery and recovery, and start working on your health and fitness in order to have a resilient body and a strong, positive mind for an excellent outcome.

  • Increase your intake of lean proteins and follow a balanced diet
  • Join the gym or follow a regular daily fitness regimen
  • Stop smoking for a few weeks before and after your tummy tuck BBL
  • Avoid the use of blood thinners such as aspirin, NSAIDs, and herbal supplements for at least two weeks before surgery
  • If you are working, make sure to apply for sufficient leave in order to have a stress-free recovery at home
  • Arrange for a responsible person who can drive you home after your surgery and preferably stay overnight to assist you
  • Obtain your pain medications, gauze bandage, antibiotics, ice packs, pillows, and compression garment in advance for your recovery
  • Ask questions and clear all doubts with your surgeon on how to prepare for abdominoplasty and BBL

How To Sleep After Tummy Tuck And BBL

In the first few days after your BBL and tummy tuck surgery, you will need to take care while sleeping so that your incision sites remain protected. Moreover, you may have some discomfort during the early healing process, so you need to know how to sleep after BBL and abdominoplasty comfortably. Here are three simple tips to help you in this process.

  • Support pillows: The type of pillow you use will make a difference while sleeping during the initial recovery phase. Ask your surgeon about specially designed supportive pillows, which you can place under your thighs as well as under your back.
  • Sleep on the side: One of the best tips on how to sleep after tummy tuck and BBL is to sleep on the side. You cannot sleep on your stomach because the abdomen needs to heal after tummy tuck, and you cannot sleep on your back because the fat transfer is performed in the buttocks. So, it is best to sleep on the sides.
  • Relaxed ambience: Your home environment and the room ambience will impact how well you can sleep during your initial recovery phase. An uncluttered and soothing bedroom setting will help you feel relaxed, and make it easier to sleep after BBL and abdominoplasty surgery.

BBL and tummy tuck pillow

When you discuss with your surgeon about how to sleep after abdominoplasty and BBL, they may suggest you to buy the right type of pillows. A specially designed BBL and tummy tuck pillow will help you get a sound, comfortable sleep during the recovery period.   • Look for inflatable wedge pillows that will rest between your lower legs to make side sleeping comfortable
  • When you want to rest or sleep on your back, use a donut-shaped circular pillow that cradles your buttocks
  • A pillow made of high quality memory foam will help lighten the pressure in the lower back and pelvic area
  • For sitting at your desk in your workplace, choose a pillow that supports your tailbone and has a non-slip bottom
  • Make sure your pillow is made from hypoallergenic materials to protect your healing skin
  • Look for the quality certifications, approvals and endorsements that the pillow manufacturer has obtained for their product
