There are two types of chemical peels: superficial (epidermal) and deep (dermal). While deep peels work on several layers of skin, superficial peels just affect the top layer of skin. Both kinds of chemical peels can be done at home with over-the-counter solutions or in a doctor’s office with stronger formulas than those available at your neighborhood pharmacy.
