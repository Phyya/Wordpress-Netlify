When Can I Do Cardio After Tummy Tuck

Cardio can be done as soon as you are cleared by your surgeon. Usually, this is at 2-3 weeks after surgery.

Cardio will help with the swelling and bruising, but it won’t speed up your healing. In fact, it may slow down your recovery because of increased blood flow to the area.

Weight lifting can also be done at 2-3 weeks post-op. However, you should not do any abdominal exercises until 6 weeks post-op. The best exercises to do during this time frame are lower body workouts such as squats, lunges and leg presses.
