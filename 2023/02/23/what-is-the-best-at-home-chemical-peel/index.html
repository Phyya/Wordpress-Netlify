What is chemical peel?

Chemical peels can help to improve the appearance of skin that has become dull and damaged by the sun, environmental pollutants and aging. A chemical peel uses a concentrated solution that burns off the top layer of skin. This treatment leaves the new skin underneath smooth, clear and even-toned.

How does it work?

The active ingredient in chemical peels is applied to the face in varying strengths depending on your skin condition and how much improvement you want. The active ingredient causes irritation on the surface of the skin, which leads to inflammation and flaking off of dead skin cells from just beneath the surface. This new layer can be smoother and clearer than before. The amount of peeling depends on how thickly or deeply you apply the solution as well as how quickly you wash it off afterwards.
