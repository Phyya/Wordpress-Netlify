Laser hair removal is a treatment that uses a laser to destroy the hair follicle, preventing it from growing back. It’s most effective on light-skinned people and those with dark hair. Laser hair removal works by targeting and destroying the root of the hair shaft, which prevents future growth.

This process can take several sessions to complete, depending on how many hairs are being treated at once and how many sessions are needed to get rid of them all. Laser treatments also leave behind an area of discoloration that disappears after a few weeks or months.

Best Type Of Laser Hair Remova Machine

There are many types of laser hair removal machines on the market. Each type has its own benefits and drawbacks. This article will help you decide which laser hair removal machine is best for you.

Not all lasers are created equal, so determining which type of laser is right for you can be difficult. Here are some things to consider when choosing a laser to remove unwanted hair:

Type of Hair: You may want to consider using a pulsed dye laser if your hair is dark or black, because this type of laser works better on darker colors than other types of lasers. If you have red or blonde hair that is not very thick, then it may be better to use a carbon dioxide (CO2) laser instead of a pulsed dye laser.

Location of Hair: Some people have more hair around the edges of their head than others. If this is true for you, then it may be better to use one of the newer models of CO2 lasers because they can be adjusted for different areas where there is more or less density in your hair follicles.

Size: Not all CO2 lasers are made alike; some are smaller in size than others and thus need to be worn closer to the skin surface while others can be worn much farther away from

Laser hair removal machines are available in different types and colors. The main difference between them is the wavelength of the light which is used in the process.

The most popular and most effective type of laser hair removal machine is called a Nd:YAG laser. This type of laser uses a wavelength of 694nm, which is similar to red light, but with a much higher intensity.

The second type of laser used for this purpose is the Alexandrite laser, which has a wavelength of 632nm. This wavelength is known as infrared light and works by killing the pigment in your skin that makes it dark and causes hair growth.

There are also other types of lasers that can be used for this purpose, such as epilators or IPL (Intense Pulsed Light).

HERE ARE 6 TIPS ON HOW TO CHOOSE THE BEST LASER HAIR REMOVAL CLINIC FOR YOU:

1. ASK WHO WILL DO OR SUPERVISE THE PROCEDURE

As mentioned earlier, although laser hair removal is an outpatient procedure, it is still a medical procedure and must be done by a licenced professional.

2. TESTIMONIALS

There may be several laser removal clinics in your area. Please make a list, check their website, and search for the customer’s comments or testimonials on their services. You may also ask friends and family if they have tried getting the procedure and ask for their comments. The more positive responses mean the clinic provides quality service to its clients. With this feedback, you can shortlist your top three clinics.

3. PROXIMITY AND TRAVEL COSTS

Since you will get multiple sessions for your treatment, consider the proximity of the clinic from your home or office. Bear in mind the travel cost, but don’t forget to factor in any possible car parking costs, as not all clinics provide free parking. But if you are looking for the best clinic, don’t be put off by driving an hour. You can thank yourself later by opting for the best clinic over the closest and most convenient one.

4. OPENING TIMES AND BOOKING PROCESS

Find out what the different clinic’s opening hours are like and how that fits in with your busy life. Does the clinic open later in the evenings so you can fit it in after work, or before the school run, or what about the weekends?

When it comes to booking, do you have easy access to a book or change an appointment, either via a phone call, the website or an online booking system?

5. TYPES OF EQUIPMENT USED

There are many types of lasers for your different needs. It would help if you asked what machine your treatment centre uses and why. A laser hair removal clinic with a wide array of new well-maintained laser machines or laser adaptors shows that the clinic provides the best care to their clients. Avoid a treatment place with outdated laser machines. Often, less effective lasers give mediocre results.

6. COSTING

After determining your top three best laser hair removal clinics, you should visit each clinic and ask for their price list. It would help if you came prepared with all your questions—from the aestheticians that will possibly perform the procedure, the clinic’s credibility, the machines used, to the clinic’s environment. From all this information, you may assess if you are getting value for your money. It is also essential to ask for their payment options.
